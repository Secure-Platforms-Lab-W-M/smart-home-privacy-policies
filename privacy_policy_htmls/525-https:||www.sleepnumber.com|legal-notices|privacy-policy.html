What do you do with my SleepIQ information? (click to read more)

We do not sell any of your SleepIQ Information or use it to target advertising.

Can I control what SleepIQ Personal Information I send? (click to read more)

Yes. You can choose to disable the transmission of SleepIQ Personal Information at any time by turning on “Privacy Mode” in the SleepIQ app. This will stop the transmission of new SleepIQ Personal Information and the only information transmitted will be an indication that the air pump is online and generally operational. With Privacy Mode on, the SleepIQ app can still be used to control your Sleep Number® setting, the bed base, and other bed features; data needed to enable these features will be processed locally at your bed and will not be transmitted to us. Some features, such as obtaining your SleepIQ score, will not be available with Privacy Mode on.
