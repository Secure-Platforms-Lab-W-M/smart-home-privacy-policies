Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2020

Rachio, Inc. (we, us, our, or Company) knows that you care about how your personal information is used and shared, and we take your privacy seriously. We have prepared the following Privacy Policy to describe how we collect, use and share personal information from users of our website (Website), our mobile application (Application), our hardware device(s) that pair or connect to the Website and/or Application (Product), and the services we provide via the Website, Application, and Product (Services). This Privacy Policy applies with respect to all information we have collected from users of our Website, Application, Product, and Services, including any information that may have been collected under prior privacy policies.

A Note About Children

We do not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from anyone under the age of 16 or knowingly allow such persons to register for the Services. If you are under 16, please do not attempt to register for the Services or send any information about yourself to us. No one under age 16 may provide any personal information to us or on the Services. In the event that we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under age 16 without verified parental consent, we will delete that information as quickly as possible. If you believe that we might have any personal information from or about a child under 16, please contact us at support@rachio.com.

What Personal Information Does Rachio Collect?

The information we gather from customers enables us to personalize and improve our services, and allows our users to set up a user account and profile that can be used to personalize their experience on the Website. We collect information from end users of the Website, Application, Product, and Services in the following ways:

Personal Information You Provide to Us:

We collect and store any information you voluntarily provide to us. For example:

  • When you register for an account on our Website or Application (Account), we will collect your name, your home address, your email address, username, and password as part of the registration process.
  • We will collect the information you provide about your irrigation system, including watering zone characteristics, watering schedules, and any pictures you upload of your yard and/or watering zones, and associate them with your Account.
  • If you tell us where your Product has been installed (e.g. by providing the city and zip code and/or latitude/longitude geo-coordinates where you have installed the product), we may store and use that information to provide you with location-based information such as weather reports relevant to your area.
  • When you order products or services on our Website, we will collect information necessary to complete the transaction, including your name, billing information, and shipping information. Our third-party payment processor may also collect your credit card information and billing information in order to process your transaction. We currently use PayPal (https://www.paypal.com) and/or Stripe, Inc. (https://stripe.com) for online payments. We do not process, record, or maintain your credit card or bank account information. For more information on how payments are handled or to understand the data security and privacy afforded such information, please refer to PayPals terms at https://www.paypal.com/us/webapps/mpp/ua/legalhub-full and privacy policy at https://www.paypal.com/us/webapps/mpp/ua/privacy-full and to Stripes terms at: https://stripe.com/us/privacy.
  • If you provide us feedback or contact us via email, we will collect your name and email address, as well as any other content included in the email, in order to send you a reply.
  • When you post messages on the message boards on our Website, the information contained in your posting will be stored on our servers and other users will be able to see it.
  • When you participate in one of our surveys, we may collect additional profile information based on the answers you provide.
  • We also collect other types of information you provide to us voluntarily, such as your Products version and registration number and other requested information if you contact us via email regarding support for the Product and Services.
  • We also collect any personal information at other points on Services that indicate that personal information is being collected.

Information that is Passively Collected when You Use the Website, Application, Product, and/or Services:

We receive and store certain types of information automatically when you interact with our Website, Application, Product, or Services. For example:

  • Log File Information. We automatically collect server log information including your IP address, cookie information, browser and/or mobile device information, operating system, timestamps, the pages you request, and the actions and path you take on the Website. Generally, we use this information to better understand how visitors to our Website use the Website and to make improvements to our Website navigation and Services.
  • Usage Information. We automatically collect information from the Website, Application, Product, and Services related to your use of the Services, including watering schedules, irrigation activity, water consumption information, alerts, skipped scheduled events, and information related to and watering zones. We use this data to better tailor our Services to our end users needs and to improve our Services.
  • Sensor Information. If you connect third-party devices to the Services, such as rain sensors, flow meters or weather stations, we may collect the information provided by these third party devices and add them to your profile information. We use this data to provide and improve the Services.
  • Mobile Services. We may also collect non-personal information from your mobile device if you have downloaded our Application, such as the features and functions of the Application you use, how you use the Application, time spent using certain features, your geographic location, and information about the type of device you use. In addition, in the event our Application crashes on your mobile device, we will receive information about your mobile device model software version and device carrier which allows us to identify and fix bugs and otherwise improve the performance of our Application.
  • Location Information. If you have enabled location services on your mobile device, we collect, with your consent, your location information in order to provide you location-based services, such as weather forecasts, so that you can adjust your irrigation system accordingly. If you do not want this information collected by us, you can disable location services on your mobile device or decline our request to collect your location information.

Information from Third Parties

We may receive information from third parties such as advertising and marketing partners, public databases, and social media platforms to supplement the information we receive from you. If we do so, this policy governs how we can use that information.

What About Cookies?

Cookies are alphanumeric identifiers that are stored on your computers (or other devices) persistent storage medium (e.g., hard drive) through your browser to enable our systems to recognize your browser and tell us how and when pages in our site are visited and by how many people. We use cookies to enhance visitors experiences, to learn more about their use of the Website and to improve the quality of our Website and Services. We may combine the general information collected through cookies with other Personal Information to tell us who you are or what your screen name or email address is.

Most browsers have an option for turning off the cookie feature, which will prevent your browser from accepting new cookies, as well as (depending on the sophistication of your browser software) allowing you to decide on acceptance of each new cookie in a variety of ways. We strongly recommend that you leave the cookies activated, however, because cookies enable you to take advantage of some of our Websites most attractive features.

Ads appearing on the Website may be delivered to users by our advertising partners, who may set cookies. These cookies allow the ad server to recognize your computer or other device each time they send you an online advertisement to compile information about you or others who use your computer or device. This information allows ad networks to, among other things, deliver targeted advertisements that they believe will be of most interest to you. This Privacy Policy covers the use of cookies by us and does not cover the use of cookies by any advertisers.

If you wish to prevent cookies from tracking your activity on our Services or visits across multiple websites, you can set your browser to block certain cookies or notify you when a cookie is set. We reserve the right to use anonymous data for any purpose and to share this data with third parties including our partners so that they, too, may provide you with an optimal online experience.

In order to enable users of the Services to learn from and share their experiences using our Products, such as watering schedules for similar climates, certain portions of the user profile information you provide to us, including your name, location, and any videos or images in your profile (Profile) may be displayed to other users of the Services. Your Account privacy settings may allow you to limit which users can see the information in your Profile. Please remember that any content you upload to your public user Profile, along with any personal information or content that you voluntarily disclose in your Profile, may be collected and used by others.

In these types of transactions, the information collected from customers and end users may be one of the business assets that are transferred. As such, we may share some or all of your personal information in connection with or during negotiation of any merger, financing, acquisition, or dissolution transaction or proceeding involving sale, transfer, divestiture, or disclosure of all or a portion of our business or assets. If another company acquires our Company, business, or assets, that company will possess the personal information collected by us and will assume the rights and obligations regarding your personal information as set forth in this Privacy Policy.

Regardless of the choices you make regarding your personal information, we may disclose your personal information if the Company believes in good faith that such disclosure is necessary: (a) to comply with relevant laws or to respond to subpoenas or warrants served on the Company; (b) enforce or investigate any potential violations of our Terms of Use, this Privacy Policy or potential violation of law; (c) in connection with a legal investigation; or (d) protect the rights, property, or safety of Company, our employees, our users, or others. This includes exchanging information with other companies and organizations for fraud protection and credit risk reduction.
  • With Your Consent. We will share your personal information in accordance with your elections.

Security of Your Personal Information

Rachio endeavors to protect user information to ensure that user account information is kept private. We use appropriate security measures to protect the security of your personal information both online and offline. These measures vary based on the sensitivity of the personal information we collect, process, and store and the current state of technology. Rachio cannot guarantee the security of user account information. Unauthorized entry or use, hardware or software failure, and other factors may compromise the security of user information at any time.

What Personal Information Can I Access?

Rachio allows you to access, update, and delete the following information in your Account:

  • Real name
  • Account and user profile information, including images and other data and information you have uploaded to the Website
  • User e-mail address
  • Location

What Choices Do I Have?

If you have any questions or comments about this notice, the ways in which Rachio collects and uses your information described this Privacy Policy, or your choices and rights regarding such use, please do not hesitate to contact us at support@rachio.com.
