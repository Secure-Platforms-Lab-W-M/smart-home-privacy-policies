What information is collected, and how is it used?

Types of information collected

When providing you with services, We will ask you for personal information that is necessary for us to successfully provide those services. If you do not provide us with this personal information, We may not be able to provide our products or services to you. We collect the following types of information (regardless of whether or not they are considered to be personal information):
  • Information you provide to us or upload: We may collect personal information you provide to us including your name, phone number, email address, account information (e.g. security-related information such as name, birthday, and gender ), information or data that is synchronized with the device you use to access the Revogi portal, information used to create your account or related to your account setup on the Revogi portal, devices you add or information that you send, and feedback.
  • Information related to the handheld device terminal or your SIM card: information related to the operation of the Revogi handheld device terminal. This includes, for example, IMEI number, IMSI number, the version of your operating system, manufacturing information about your device, model name, and network operator.
  • Device information: information about the device associated with your account. For example: MAC address, DID, network status of the device (IP/network signal), and the version of your firmware.
  • Information We designate that is about you: We may collect and use content such as information related to your account.
  • Location information (only for specific services/features): a variety of different kinds of information related to your location. For example, country code, city code, mobile network code, district name, information about latitude and longitude, and language settings.
  • Login information: information related to your use of certain features and devices. Information includes, for example, cookie and other anonymous labeling and marking technology, IP address, temporary messaging history, and standard system logs.
  • Other information: Environmental Feature Value (ECV) (for device-bound accounts (email and phone number), account number and password for connected Wi-Fi accounts)
We may also collect other kinds of anonymous information that is not related to an individual. For example, when using a specific service, a user's device, device model number, and the version number of the user's firmware may all be collected. The type and amount of information collected depends on how you use, join, or participate in our products and/or services.

How this personal information is used

The purpose of collecting personal information is to provide you with products and/or services while also ensuring that We comply with applicable law. You hereby agree to our processing of personal information in accordance with the purposes stipulated by this Privacy Policy, and you agree to disclose personal information to our affiliates (these may include communications, social media, technology, or cloud services) and to third-party service providers (as defined below). We may use your personal information for the following purposes:
  • Provider services, processing, maintenance, improvement, development of the products and/or services We provide you, and services provided through your device or by Revogi.
  • Communication with you regarding your device, services, or any other common queries (including updates, device firmawer/software updates, customer support, related information, and notifications)
  • Analysis and development of statistical information related to the use of our products and services that is used to further improve our products and services.
  • Storage and maintenance of your information, to be used in our business operations or to fulfill legal obligations.
More details on how We use your information (including personal information) are provided below:
  • Creating your account. The personal information that is collected when an account is created on a mobile device is used to create a user's personal account and information pages.
  • Providing location-based services. When using Revogi devices, We may use information about your location to determine the time zone of the device. This will ensure that the device accurately displays the correct time and login service area. It will also optimize user experience by providing services such as advance activation of the corresponding device at a time set by the user. You can turn off this feature at any time by going to device settings or by discontinuing your use of this application.
  • Accessing devices. The authenticity of devices that are operated through the app must be verified. We therefore need to confirm the legal identity of the device by consulting the factory MAC and DID information. In addition, in order to connect the device to the network, it will need to be configured through the app. During configuration, you must enter the corresponding Wi-Fi name and password. This information will only be used to connect the device to the network. The Wi-Fi and password data you enter will be encrypted and stored locally on your device; it will not be uploaded to the server. In addition, you can access the device and delete this information at any time. We also need to analyze the device's IP and network signal in order to determine the optimal network connection method. This will ensure the stability of the device network.
  • Display of device status. This allows you to view the status of the device remotely that you can check at any time whether or not the device is running.
  • Providing push services. Account numbers and IMEO Numbers will also be used for the Revogi push service, which sends device notifications to the user. You can turn off this feature at any time by changing the preferences listed under "Message Settings."
  • Collecting user feedback. The feedback you choose to provide is extremely valuable, as it helps us improve the services provided by Revogi. In order to track your feedback, Revogi may use the personal information you provide to contact you and to keep records.
  • Sending notifications. We may occasionally use your personal information to send important notifications, for example, notifications about the status of device malfunctions; your customized device push notifications (which depend on what additional equipment you have purchased); and changes to our terms, conditions, and policies.
