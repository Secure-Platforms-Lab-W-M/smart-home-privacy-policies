Privacy policy for homee usage

All data is stored locally on the device, therefore we do not collect any personal data. After user consent, homee collects anonymous usage statistics (homee analytics), which are used to improve the product continuously. To enable remote access to the device, data is sent encrypted over the internet. Data transmission over the internet has possible security vulnerabilities. We are using industry standard security mechanisms to provide a high level of security, total security, however, cant be guaranteed.
