Privacy Policy

Last updated on February 24, 2020.

When you install our Minut Device in your home or other residential property, it collects data that allows you to monitor your home or other residential properties for variables such as noise level, humidity, temperature and alarm recognition without intrusive cameras. That data is uploaded wirelessly to our Minut Mobile App that you have downloaded on your mobile device. If your purchase includes the Online Dashboard (see below), that data is also available to you in the Online Dashboard. In addition, when you interact with the Minut Mobile App or the Online Dashboard, purchase a Minut Device or register as a customer – or when you browse our Websites – certain information about you is collected. The purpose of this Privacy Policy is to help you understand what data and information we collect and how we use it, so please take a moment to review it. Privacy is an important matter in the digital age – and it has become increasingly complicated to understand who collects information about you and how it is used.

First you need to understand what this Privacy Policy relates to. When we use the terms “we”, “our”, or “us” in this Privacy Policy, we mean our company called Minut, Inc. and its subsidiaries and affiliates worldwide. This Privacy Policy applies to our Minut device (“Minut Device”), our website located at minut.com and any other website we may establish in the future (individually a “Website” and collectively “Websites”), the Minut mobile application developed by us and any other app we may develop in the future (each, a “Minut Mobile App” and collectively the “Minut Mobile Apps”), the multi-property online dashboard (the “Online Dashboard”) for those of you whose purchase includes the dashboard for managing multiple properties, and also any other software, cloud service, add-on service or feature developed by us and available as part of the Service whether now existing or incorporated in the future. By using any of the Services, you accept that we may use the data and information we collect from you in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

What data do we collect?

Personal Data

Personal Data is information that identifies a particular individual (“Personal Data”). In order for us to provide the Services to you, we need to collect and use certain Personal Data about you. We may collect the following Personal Data when you order a Minut Device, interact with our customer service, add another authorized user for your Minut Device, or otherwise use any of the Services:

  • Your and the other authorized users’ first and last names.
  • Your and the other authorized users’ e-mail address, telephone number, address and other contact information.
  • The GPS coordinates of your home or other residential property where you have installed your Minut Device(s).
  • Your and the other authorized users’ photographs (if you have chosen to upload photographs to the Minut Mobile App).

When you pay for your Minut Device(s) and for any other fees related to your use of the Minut Device (such as subscription fees) on our Website or from one of our third party vendors, you will need to provide your credit card or other applicable payment method. We do not collect this information because we use third parties to process payments for Minut Devices. When making a payment, you should refer to the Privacy Policy of our payment processors. We currently use stripe.com, PayPal and Shopify to process payments and may add additional payment processors in the future. Third party vendors may use other payment processors.

Sensor Data

Sensor Data is information that a Minut Device detects and gathers from your home, such as the level of temperature, humidity and noise. You will be able to decide in your settings in the Minut Mobile App or Online Dashboard, as applicable, at what level of temperature, humidity or noise you will want your Minut Device to send a separate alert to your Minut Mobile App or the Online Dashboard, as applicable. Your Minut Device does not record conversations.

Special Note About Children. Our Services are not directed or suitable for children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect any Personal Data from children under 18 without parental consent. If you are aware of a user under the age of 18 using the Services without parental consent, please contact us immediately. We reserve the right to delete or deny access to any account or authorized user immediately if we suspect the account holder or authorized user is a child under the age of 18 whether the child is using the Services with or without parental consent.

Please note that if you add your child under the age of 18 as an authorized user of your Minut Device and the Minut Service, you will be deemed to have given your parental consent to the inclusion of your child’s Personal Data in the Services. If you add someone else’s child (such as a relative staying with you) as an authorized user of your Minut Device and Minut Service, you must get that child’s parental consent. Use caution when adding an underaged child’s Personal Data (such as the name and photograph) in the Services. Perhaps consider substituting a photograph of your child with a picture of an inanimate object. Please also read the Section titled “What else should I know about security and the Minut Service?” below.

What else should I know about security and the Services?

Sharing information and having real-time access to data about your home is fun and easy, but also brings along some risks that you should be aware of so that you can enjoy your Minut Device responsibly.

Please note that it is your responsibility to determine what action you will take as a result of the Sensor Data detected from your home or any alerts that Minut Device sends to your Minut Mobile App or the Online Dashboard, as applicable. Our Minut Device is not able to determine the cause of any variations in the Sensor Data detected and will not, for example, alert a home security service or the authorities as a result of any data measured or alarm detected unless you have purchased a separate first response service from us as an add-on. Such first response services are currently available only in select locations and are also subject to additional terms and conditions provided separately.

You should be mindful of the security of your computer or mobile device, and prevent unauthorized third parties from opening your Minut Mobile App and Online Dashboard. We recommend you protect your computer and mobile device with a passcode and change your Minut Mobile App’s and Online Dashboard’s log-in password periodically. If you have given access to your Minut Mobile App or Online Dashboard to a third party (such as a friend in order to monitor your home while you’re traveling), we recommend you change your password after you no longer need the third party to monitor your home and also terminate such third party’s access to your Minut Mobile App or Online Dashboard.

Please use caution when setting up your Minut Mobile App and Online Dashboard, and consider the potential ramifications to you, your family and your guests should your Minut Mobile App or Online Dashboard end up in the wrong hands. We do not wish to scare you, but do want to help you understand our technology better. For example, if you set up your Minut Mobile App or Online Dashboard to show whether someone is home or not – and an unauthorized person gains acces to your Minut Mobile App or Online Dashboard – that person would be able to see where your home/property is and that your home/property is empty when nobody’s home. If you do not wish to track the “at home/not at home” feature of your Minut account’s authorized users, do not activate that feature in the Minut Mobile App or the Online Dashboard, and be sure to turn off location services for the Minut Mobile App in everyone’s mobile device. That might also be a good opportunity for you to check which third party applications on your mobile device are tracking your location – and turn location services off for those apps if necessary.

The use of our Services requires an Internet connection and your Minut Device requires a wi-fi Internet connection. You are responsible for obtaining your own wi-fi and Internet connection to use your Minut Device and our Services. You should understand that your mobile phone, computer and other electronic devices are susceptible to attack from third parties. We recommend you use firewalls, store your passwords in a safe place, and create passwords for yourself that are strong. The security of your Internet connection and/or wi-fi network is your responsibility and out of our control.
