4. Cookies and similar technologies
We use Cookies and other similar technologies, such as Web Beacons (Web Beacons) to provide this service:

4,1 Cookies
Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device for recording information. We can not access your personal data by Cookies. We only use Cookies to bring you the following convenient functions:

  1. Login and Verification: Cookies remember your login and verification information to help you visit the site and web content of this Service.
  2. Save your preferences: Cookies can store your preferences on your device, such as the language category or country you are accustomed to using; then you will not need to choose country and language the next time you visit this service website again.
  3. Statistics and Use Analysis: Cookies record the number and duration of your visit to or use of the Service. We can use the above information to compute statistics to understand the performance status of the Service, has effectively adjust or develop new services or functions.
  4. Customized advertising offers: Cookies can record your usage and interest, such as the type of ad you've viewed on this Service, from this record our partners will provide the marketing content that you are interested in.

The Cookies we use for this service are divided into the following categories. From the following table, you can find out the Cookies that we use in this Service:

The first type -efficiency Cookies
This type of Cookie is used to collect information about the way you use this Service, such as the type of page you are most browsing or whether you receive an error message on the page. This type of Cookies does not collect information that identifies you, and all the data collected by this type of Cookies will be collected and edited in a manner of non-identification and irreversible. This type of Cookies is only used to optimize the Service.

The second type- functional Cookies
This type of Cookies is used to let us record the choices you make when you visit the service website, such as the language type you choose to use or where you are located to provide you with more of your preferred features.

The third type- Targeted or Advertising Cookies
Cookies of this type will provide you with marketing information that fits your interests by recording your use of the service website. Cookies of this type will also be used to limit the number of times you see an ad and measure the effectiveness of a campaign.
