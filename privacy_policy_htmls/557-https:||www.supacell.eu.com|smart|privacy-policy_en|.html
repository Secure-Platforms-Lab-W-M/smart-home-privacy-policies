SUPACELL PRIVACY POLICY

Multibrands International Ltd. is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy (“Policy”) describes our practices in connection with information privacy on Personal Data we process through your individual use of the following services, products, and related mobile applications (collectively, the “Products”):

– Supacell Smart Mobile Application

Our app Supacell Smart, in conjunction with the device user interface, possesses the ability to transmit such data through a wireless network. Together, the app allows access and control with our Smart devices. If you choose to use our service, then you agree to the collection and use of such information relating to this policy

Personal information is any kind of data that could be used to identify an individual or entity. It includes but is not limited to –

Email address

Device ID

First and last name

Billing or shipping information

Multibrands International Ltd shall collect and use information such as 1) You Voluntarily Provide Us (Account or Profile Data, Feedback) 2) Information We Collect Automatically (Device Information, Usage Data, Log Information, Location Information) 3) Smart Devices Related Information (Basic Information of Smart Devices, Information Reported by Smart Devices)  to authenticate a legitimate user by relying on the terms of the European Union’s General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and to provide feedback while addressing any problem. Our app Supacell collects and uses such personal information with the consent of a user or as otherwise permitted in law. In case we are not permitted to use and store the data, we shall delete the personal data as soon as you delete or deactivate the user account.

  • Marketing Communication: We may process your Personal Data to provide marketing and promotional materials to you on our Products and Services. Multibrands International Ltd usually does not use cookies explicitly. However, the app may use third party code and libraries that use cookies to collect information and improve services. Users have the option to either accept or refuse these cookies and to know when a cookie is being sent to your device. If a user chooses to refuse cookies, he/she may not be able to use some portion of our services. Cookies are a small amount of data used by the browser from the websites that a user visits and are stored on the device’s internal memory. Sometimes Multibrands International Ltd may also use the log data to know the time and date of your use of service.

Multibrands International Ltd values your trust in providing us with your personal information, thus we are striving to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. But we must not forget that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure and reliable.
