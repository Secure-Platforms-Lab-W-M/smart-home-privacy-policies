Description of services

                        

                            

                                                                        
Via technical hardware, Applications control the associated Connected devices, therefore enabling you to control the comfort, consumption and safety in your home. They also allow for multimedia equipment, lighting, heating, shutters, alarms and video surveillance to be remotely controlled.
An automatic opening and closing of shutters is made available through up and down times of the sun. This prevents users from technical programming steps. To achieve this though, localization data is needed for an adequate and precise User experience. This data is solely used for this unique functionality of automatically programming up and down times of shutters. This data is stored only on the Tydom gateway and never stored in the cloud.
In regards to video surveillance, data is stored in keeping with the applicable legal dispositions in the User’s country in order to guarantee continuity of the services offered.
